About SPECTRO Protocol

SPECTRO is an educational initiative designed to demystify Intent-Centric Design and cross-chain interoperability. Our focus is on providing clear, technical insights into how solvers and EIP-712 compliance are shaping the future of decentralized finance.

Interested in the technical mechanics? Explore our deep dives on the Intents page.

Mission

To bridge the gap between complex blockchain infrastructure and user-friendly outcomes through the study of Intents and Abstraction.

Vision

A decentralized web where users focus on what they want to achieve, while professional solvers handle how it happens.

Values

Security-first development, cryptographic transparency via EIP-712, and trustless execution models.
