Protocol Core Pillars

Intent-Centric Design

A paradigm shift where users define specific outcomes, delegating the execution logic to a specialized network of solvers.

EIP-712 Compliance

Enhanced security through human-readable typed data signing, ensuring full transparency before transaction authorization.

Solver Liquidity

Professional agents compete to fulfill intents off-chain, providing immediate capital and optimized execution rates.

Cross-Chain Settlement

Seamless value transfer across disparate networks, abstracting the complexity of traditional bridging mechanisms.

Deterministic Nonces

Advanced anti-replay protection utilizing unique cryptographic identifiers to maintain global state integrity.

Verifiable Proofs

Cryptographic validation of state conditions, ensuring that rewards are only released upon successful intent fulfillment.
